 Proteins are large biomolecules, or
 macromolecules, consisting of one
 or more long chains of amino acid
 residues. Proteins perform a vast
 array of functions within organisms,
 including catalysing metabolic reactions,
 DNA replication, responding to stimuli,
 providing structure to cells and
 organisms, and transporting molecules
 from one location to another.
